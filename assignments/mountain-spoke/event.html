Cycle the Continental Divide Annual Event

Save the date for the 2019 Cycle the Continental Divide Ride on June 15, 2019! This unique event features mountain-based routes that allow cyclists to discover beautiful landscapes while riding around the different trails selected in the Flathead national Forest from Montana within an enjoyable, supportive atmosphere. In the heart of the Rocky Mountains, west of the continental divide and just south of the Canadian border, lies the 2.4 million acre Flathead National Forest. Three routes are offered: 75, 50 and 25 miles. Turn by turn, the Cycle the Continental Divide Ride connects cyclists with numerous lakes, streams, and rivers to enjoy. Prices are $50, $70, and $95 respectively. Are you signed up? Fill out the form below to enter.
